About

Built by one person.
Designed for everyone.

Aorbub is an independent suite of business tools built by Huzefa Haveliwala — a product builder based in Kuwait with a background in software testing, business operations, and AI-assisted development.

Each tool in the Aorbub suite solves a real business problem — from managing inventory and tracking churn to making smarter pricing and product decisions.

Built using React, Firebase, and modern AI development tools. Aorbub is an ongoing project with more tools planned.
